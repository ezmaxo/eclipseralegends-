Game Overview

Product Overview

Eclipsera Legends is a blockchain-based Play-to-Earn RPG gacha game built on the Solana network. The game combines immersive RPG gameplay with NFT-based digital ownership, competitive battles, and a player-driven economy.

Core Features

Gameplay Modes

  • PvE (Player vs Environment): Fight monsters, collect resources, and level up heroes. Earn items, crystals, and tokens for progression.
  • PvP (Player vs Player): Turn-based battles with USDT wagers. Winners receive token rewards and resources; leaderboard and seasonal rewards promote competition.
  • MVM (Multiplayer vs Monster / Planetary Warfare): Teams stake SOL to attack or defend planets. Real-time rewards and risk/reward mechanics drive strategic play.

Land & Building System

  • Land Ownership: Players acquire and upgrade land to generate daily ECS tokens.
  • Buildings: Towers, Wells, Banks, Hunting Lodges, and Support structures enhance land utility and defense.
  • Mining Factories: Unlockable factories generate stronger rewards based on ECS token holdings.

NFT Assets

Heroes, weapons, and rare items are tokenized as NFTs. NFTs enhance gameplay performance, can be traded on marketplaces, and introduce scarcity-driven value.

Token Economy

  • ECS Token: Utility token for buying items, heroes, land upgrades, and participating in PvP/MVM.
  • Presale & Public Sale: Early access incentivizes adoption and liquidity.
  • Staking & Reward Pools: Players earn daily/weekly rewards for holding ECS tokens while actively participating in gameplay.

Competitive & Social Features

  • Guild System: Players form guilds for cooperative gameplay, exclusive events, and collective rewards.
  • Battle Pass & Referral System: Seasonal subscription and referral incentives increase engagement and retention.
  • Leaderboard & Seasonal Rewards: Tracks wins, losses, and rankings for both PvP and MVM battles.

Player-Centric Economy

Players can earn real rewards in ECS tokens and SOL. Assets, land, and achievements have tangible value via NFT ownership and marketplace trading.

Vision

To create a sustainable, player-driven ecosystem where fun gameplay, strategy, and digital ownership converge, delivering real rewards and long-term engagement.
